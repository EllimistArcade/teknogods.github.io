Game Info: Initial D Arcade Stage 8 Infinity âˆž was released July 17, 2014. Like its predecessor, this game carries over all game modes from the previous version with a notable addition of Initial D Factory and D Coins. In Story Mode, the players can play the Team Side or Rival Side to see Driving Training Curiculum or what mission is for each story. The game added a new feature "1 Day 1 Time Continue" to allow players to have a free continue per day without inserting credits. New courses such as Momiji Line, for the first time in Special Stage, also appeared with new layout and Hakone, the place for battle between Ryosuke's FC and Rin Hojo's R32. Arcade Stage Ver.3/Street Stage characters such as Miki, Atsuro Kawai and The Lan Evo Teams also return.
